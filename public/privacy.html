Privacy

Mothtrap · last updated 18 July 2026

Mothtrap is a client for Bluesky, run as a personal research project. It is a static web app: your browser talks to Bluesky directly, and there is no Mothtrap account, database, or user record anywhere.

What stays on your device

Almost everything. Mothtrap keeps the following in your own browser storage:

  • An archive of posts your feed has shown you, in IndexedDB (skynets-archive-<your-did>), so the graph can be rebuilt without refetching.
  • The list of posts you've dismissed.
  • Your display settings and which feed tab you last used.
  • Your login session, if you signed in with an app password.

None of that is transmitted to us. Clearing your browser storage for this site erases all of it permanently — there is no server-side copy to restore from.

What leaves your device

The topic digest sends post text to a server we run. This is the one place Mothtrap is not purely local, and it is worth reading carefully.

Mothtrap groups your feed into topics using a small language model. On mothtrap.blue that model runs on our server, so generating a digest sends it:

  • the text of the posts currently in your graph (truncated, up to a few hundred characters each);
  • for replies and quotes, the text of the post being replied to or quoted.

No handles, names or account identifiers are sent — not yours, and not those of the people you follow. Labelling a post is a summary of what it is about, so who wrote it is irrelevant to the task and is never transmitted.

Each post is sent once, to be given a short topic label, and the label is then kept on your device — so re-opening Mothtrap doesn't send the same post again.

It is sent to generate short topic labels, is processed in memory to answer that request, and is then gone. Neither the web server nor the model service records the content of these requests: the web server logs only the usual line (time, IP, and the path requested) and the model service logs only that a request happened and how long it took. Nothing is retained for analysis, used for training, or passed to any third party. The model is self-hosted — nothing goes to a commercial AI provider.

Grouping the resulting topics into conversations needs text embeddings, and those are computed entirely inside your browser — the model is downloaded once, on first use, and runs on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere for that step.

If you run Mothtrap locally you can point it at your own machine's model instead, or at a provider of your choosing with your own API key, in which case that provider's terms apply and nothing reaches us at all.

Bluesky and other services

  • Your credentials go to Bluesky, never to us. Signing in with OAuth happens on Bluesky's own site. If you use an app password instead, your browser sends it directly to your Bluesky server to exchange for a session token, which is then stored only on your device.
  • Reading your timeline, posting, liking, following, muting, blocking and reporting are all requests from your browser to your Bluesky server.
  • Avatars and image thumbnails load from Bluesky's CDN, which will see your IP address, as any website's images would.
  • Reports you submit are sent to the moderation service for your Bluesky account. Mothtrap does not receive or review them.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising, and no sale or sharing of data.
  • No third-party scripts or embedded trackers.
  • No cookies used to identify or follow you.
  • No accounts, so nothing to profile you with.

Our web server keeps ordinary request logs — IP address, time, and the file requested, as essentially every web server does. They are rotated daily and kept for 14 days, are used only to keep the site running, record no request content, and are not connected to any Bluesky identity.

Your control

  • Sign out to remove your session from this device.
  • Clear this site's browser storage to delete the archive, dismissals and settings outright.
  • Your posts, follows, mutes and blocks live in your Bluesky account, not in Mothtrap — manage or delete them there and Mothtrap reflects it.
  • Don't open the digest and no post text is ever sent anywhere.

Children

Mothtrap shows unfiltered content from a public social network and is not intended for children.

Changes

If this policy changes in a way that affects what leaves your device, the date at the top will change and the change will be described here.
