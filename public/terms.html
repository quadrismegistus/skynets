Mothtrap · version 1 · 18 July 2026

Mothtrap is an independent client for Bluesky, built as a research project by one person. It draws your existing timeline as a map of conversations. Using it means agreeing to what follows.

Zero tolerance for abuse and objectionable content

There is no tolerance here for objectionable content or abusive behaviour. Not for harassment, threats, hate speech, or content that is illegal or sexually exploitative — and least of all for anything involving children.

By using Mothtrap you agree that you will not use it to:

  • post or share content that is abusive, harassing, hateful, threatening, or illegal;
  • target, stalk, or intimidate anyone;
  • post sexual content involving minors, or any content that exploits a child;
  • impersonate other people, or misrepresent who you are;
  • evade someone's block, or use the app to circumvent anyone's moderation choices.

How that is enforced

Every post carries report, mute and block in its ⋯ menu. When you report something, Mothtrap submits that report to the moderation service for your Bluesky account — the service with the power to remove a post or suspend an account. That submission is a real action taken on your behalf, not a suggestion that you go and do it elsewhere.

Mothtrap also honours the outcomes: it applies the content labels, mutes and blocks on your account, hiding or covering posts accordingly, and it can hide replies to accounts you have silenced.

What it cannot do, and will not pretend to: Mothtrap displays Bluesky, it does not host it. The posts live on Bluesky's servers, so deciding a report and removing a post or account is theirs to do. Mothtrap gets your report to them and respects the result.

If you believe Mothtrap is failing to respect a label, mute or block, that is a bug and I want to hear about it — see the contact page. Concerns sent there get a reply, normally within a few working days.

Not for children

Mothtrap shows unfiltered content from a public social network, including content that may be unsuitable for minors. It is not intended for children, and you should not use it if you are under 17.

Your Bluesky account

You need your own Bluesky account, and Bluesky's own terms and community guidelines continue to apply to everything you post. Mothtrap holds no account of its own for you; your posts, follows, mutes and blocks live in your Bluesky account and can be managed there.
